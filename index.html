What is Valorant?

Valorant is a competive first person shooter which takes elements from other games such as Overwatch and CSGO. It was made by Riot games who have also made games like League of Legends. It came out last year in april.

Why I like Valorant

Not only can I play with my friends, but it brings a sense of competitiveness that makes the game super enjoyable.
